❓ COMMON QUESTIONS
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Q: Why is accuracy 99% if high-risk recall is only 89%?
  A: Dataset is imbalanced. 99% accuracy includes well-predicted low-risk
     cases. See LEARNING_GUIDE.md Q3 for detailed explanation.

  Q: What does uncertainty mean?
  A: Ensemble disagreement. If models disagree → uncertain prediction.
     See VISUAL_GUIDE.md "Uncertainty Estimation Mechanism" for example.

  Q: How do I use this in production?
  A: Three options (REST API, Docker, or direct import).
     See README.md "Deployment Options" and respiratory_agent_api.py

  Q: What if data is missing?
  A: Handled automatically (median imputation for numeric, mode for categorical).
     See NOTEBOOK_EXPLANATION.md Section 4.

  Q: Can I retrain the model?
  A: Yes! The notebook is designed for retraining.
     See LEARNING_GUIDE.md Q8 for instructions.
